Research project

Comprehensive Information System for Polish Metrology

The project develops a coherent concept for an information system supporting metrology data, processes and services within the National Quality Infrastructure and public statistics system.

About the project Contact
Scope
Metrology, data, interoperability and quality infrastructure
Output
Data architecture, information model and implementation recommendations
Users
Public administration, laboratories, industry and research institutions

Mission

Organising information, data and processes of Polish Metrology

The project focuses on practical foundations of a digital metrology ecosystem: a shared data language, responsible information sharing and linking resources with user needs.

The work builds on earlier research into the economic impact of metrology, which revealed data gaps affecting monitoring, evaluation and evidence-based policy design. The project links metrology, the National Quality Infrastructure, data ecosystems and public statistics.
