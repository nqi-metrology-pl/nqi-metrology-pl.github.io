How the team works

The team works in an expert and analytical model. This combines document review, stakeholder interviews, information modelling and recommendations for later stages of digital metrology services.

The team composition reflects the project assumption that an information system for Polish Metrology requires economic, statistical, technical and organisational competences at the same time.
