News

Presentations and discussions on project results

Project work has been presented and discussed at national and international conferences focused on statistics, data ecosystems, data spaces and public information governance.

August 2025

Data space concept presented at the ASA Joint Statistical Meeting in Nashville

At the ASA Joint Statistical Meeting 2025, the project presented the concept of a National Quality Infrastructure data space as a case study for discussing the future of common data spaces. The panel addressed the role of the statistical community in design, governance and quality assurance.

2025

Project discussed at the Polish Statistics Congress in Warsaw

At the Polish Statistics Congress, project experience was used to discuss data spaces in public administration. The discussion covered technical, legal, organisational and social challenges relevant to modern information environments.

18-20 November 2024

Project results presented at Multivariate Statistical Analysis 2024

At the University of Lodz, the team presented conclusions from project work on new European regulations in data governance. Particular attention was given to the importance of European data spaces for designing public sector information systems.

11-14 November 2024

Participation in the UN World Data Forum in Medellin

During the United Nations World Data Forum 2024, the project was represented in a panel discussion on public-private partnerships and modern information environments. The discussion addressed data gaps, statistical capacity, ethical data use and the strengthening of data ecosystems.

3-8 August 2024

Project work presented at the ASA Joint Statistical Meeting in Portland

At the international conference in Portland, the project presented issues related to the role of data and information stewards in designing and maintaining advanced information systems. The presentation helped confront project assumptions with the experience of statistics and data science experts.
